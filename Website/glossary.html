Cultural Glossary

Key terms to help you navigate Korean culture and travel.

Hanbok

Traditional Korean clothing worn for formal occasions, festivals, and celebrations. Characterized by vibrant colors and simple lines without pockets.

Kimchi

A staple traditional side dish of salted and fermented vegetables, such as napa cabbage and Korean radish, made with a widely varying selection of seasonings.

T-Money

A rechargeable smart card used for paying transportation fares in and around Seoul and other areas of South Korea.

Bibimbap

A Korean rice dish. The term literally means "mixed rice". It is served as a bowl of warm white rice topped with seasoned vegetables.
